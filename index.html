Professinal Areas & Research Interests

Biography

    Anthony is a Musculoskeletal Tissue Biomechanist Research Fellow

    Anthony received his BS in Chemistry from Wofford College in 2015, and his MS in Bioengineering from Oregon State University in 2020, working with Ravi Balasubramanian and Jim Sweeney.
